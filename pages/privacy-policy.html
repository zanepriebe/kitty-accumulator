Kitty Accumulator Privacy Policy

At Fredno Studios, we care about your privacy as much as you do. The only user data we collect within Kitty Accumulator is used to enable Google Play achievements and leaderboards. To enable these features, we use your Google account credentials to retrieve your achievements and leaderboard information and update it as needed. We don't sell this data and we don't save it. As soon as you quit the app, this data is destroyed.

By prioritizing user privacy, we hope to make Kitty Accumulator a fun and safe experience for all ages. Thanks for taking the time to read our privacy policy and happy accumulating!
